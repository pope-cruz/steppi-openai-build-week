# Steppi Product Context

> Supporting design context only. `docs/SPEC.md` remains the implementation
> contract and `docs/VISION.md` remains the product-direction source of truth.

## Register

product

## Users

Steppi serves high-school students discovering career roles and possible fields
of study, and college students connecting their courses, experiences, and
changing interests to career possibilities. Recurring interface copy should use
“students” unless naming both groups materially improves clarity.

## Product Purpose

Steppi is a career exploration tool. It helps students see more possibilities,
understand what the work is like, and decide what they want to explore next. AI
supports this process by connecting student context to varied roles; it is not a
counsellor, predictor, or authority.

## Brand Personality

Warm, calm, credible, youthful, and exploratory.

## Anti-references

Steppi must not resemble an aptitude test, ranked recommendation engine,
corporate dashboard, generic AI chatbot, admissions planner, or job-placement
service. Avoid “perfect match,” “best fit,” prediction, diagnosis, and language
that implies one correct future.

## Design Principles

- Present possibilities rather than predictions.
- Teach what roles involve before discussing long-term pathways.
- Keep the student in control of Steppi’s interpretation.
- Connect education to career literacy without becoming a college planner.
- Use current sources only when a question requires unstable external facts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use plain, stage-neutral language that works for students in high school or
college. Maintain keyboard access, visible focus, semantic labels, sufficient
contrast, reduced-motion support, and calm actionable failure states.
